Data Cleaning Summary :-

Missing Values
Checked all columns and found no missing values.
Date columns contained time information, but all timestamps were 00:00:00, providing no additional value.
Converted date columns to proper datetime format and removed the unnecessary time component to simplify analysis.

Duplicate Records
Checked for duplicate rows and found no duplicate records in the dataset.

Outlier Analysis
Performed outlier detection using the IQR method.
No significant outliers were found in Order ID, Units Sold, Unit Price, and Unit Cost.
A large number of outliers were detected in Total Revenue, Total Cost, and Total Profit. These values were retained because they represent legitimate large sales transactions and are common in sales datasets.
Outlier treatment was not applied since removing them could lead to the loss of important business information.
